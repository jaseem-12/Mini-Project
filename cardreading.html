Card Reading System

  • Approach the bus at the starting point and locate the card reader near the entrance.
  • Swipe or tap the smart card on the reader to board the bus.
  • The system deducts the appropriate fare as you board, ensuring a cashless and efficient process.
  • Your entry onto the bus is recorded in real-time, contributing to passenger tracking and management.
  • During the journey, the smart card serves as your travel pass, and there's no need to interact with the card reader.
  • When reaching the destination or ending point, approach the exit and find the card reader near the door.
  • Swipe or tap the smart card on the reader to register your exit from the bus
  • The system adjusts the fare based on your journey, deducting the appropriate amount for the distance traveled.
