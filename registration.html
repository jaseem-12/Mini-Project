Instructions For Using Smart card

Step1 :Get Your Smart Card Activation Code Online

  • visit smartcard.in
  • Go To Regitsration Poratl and Get Your Activation Code
  • Enter your personal details exactly as they are mentioned on your Aadhaar card
Your Aadhaar details will be instantly verified online, and your unique card activation code will be generated.

Step2 :Get A New Smart Card At Any SmartCard Centre

Now you can visit any SmartCard Centre with your activation code and get a new Smart Card.

Step3 :Board the bus and tap the smartcard on the designated card reader when you enter.

Step4 :The fare will be deducted automatically from the card balance.

Step5 :You may need to tap the card again when you exit the bus, for calculating fare
