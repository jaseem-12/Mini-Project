The New Way Of Bus Travel


In your City


Smart card system is a contactless smart card used for fare payment on public transportation system. This smart card serves as an electronic ticket that allows passengers to access and pay for various mode of public transportation. It, mean’s passengers do not need to physically insert the card in to a machine. Instead, they simply tap the card on card reader located at entry and exit points of transit stations and vehicles. They reduce the need for paper tickets, cash payments and waiting in long queues. Travelers can easily manage their accounts online and even set up auto top-up to ensure they always have enough credit. Smart card has revolutionized how people pay for and access public transportation, making it a convenient, cost effective, and efficient system for millions of commuters and visitors to the city.
